# SpeakEasy AAC App

**Give a voice to your loved ones with SpeakEasy AAC.**

SpeakEasy AAC is a simple, fully customizable Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) app designed to help individuals who have difficulty speaking communicate effectively. Whether for a toddler learning their first words, a non-verbal child with Autism, or an adult recovering from a stroke, SpeakEasy provides a clean, distraction-free interface to express needs and thoughts.

Unlike expensive communication devices or complex apps, SpeakEasy is designed to be **intuitive, offline, and completely private.**
